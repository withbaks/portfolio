Get to know more about me

Korede Bakare is a UI/UX Designer from Nigeria, focused on improving human-computer interaction across industries. With a Computer Science degree from Chrisland University and a strong interest in Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), he has led projects like an AI shopping assistant that brings the interactive, social feel of in-store shopping to online platforms. As the Head of UX Design at Avniverse, Korede improved their user experience score by 30%. He actively shares insights on design, startups, and innovation, inspiring and empowering his community to shape the next generation of innovators. He has also explored Frontend development, backend development, AI, machine learning, cybersecurity, and computer hardware, adding depth to his problem-solving and technical knowledge.

Korede thrives in dynamic, collaborative environments and enjoys mentoring young designers as a Chrisland University UX Design Club alumnus. Currently, he is working on experimental projects in the health industry.
