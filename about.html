Here is a little more about me

Korede Bakare is a User Experience Designer from Nigeria, merging his technical and creative abilities to enhance human-computer interactions. With a degree in Computer Science from Chrisland University, he has led impactful projects like an AI-driven multimodal shopping assistant that replicates the interactive benefits of in-store assistants by personalizing the online shopping experience.

His career highlights include serving as Head of UX Design at Trade-X and leading UX at Payvry, where he improved user-to-vendor transactions by 30%. Korede believes that great design not only solves problems but also inspires and connects people. He regularly shares insights on design, startups, and innovation, fostering a community of future innovators.

Korede thrives in dynamic, collaborative environments and enjoys mentoring young designers as a Chrisland University UX Design Club alumnus. Currently, he is working on experimental projects in the medical industry.
